About Me


Kiara Neve Perez has an extensive background in guest service, having worked at BURGERFI for a year and a half and Universal Orlando Resort for over two years. She is dedicated to her work and goal oriented. She was in band for nine years and was involved in her high school band’s leadership. After graduating from Lake Mary High School, Kiara continued her education focusing on economics and business. There, at Seminole State College, she participated in a leadership retreat.


Kiara enjoys reading for enjoyment, but also reading for knowledge. She takes pleasure in new learning experiences. She is now a student in the University of Central Florida’s coding boot camp, learning to code HTML, CSS, and Javascript. She has a passion for travelling, meeting new people, and experiencing new cultures.
